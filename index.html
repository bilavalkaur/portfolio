About Me

I am a junior engineering student at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala, with a strong focus on chemical engineering and its practical applications in solving real-world problems. My educational journey has equipped me with robust leadership and team management skills, which I actively apply in my academic and extracurricular pursuits.
I am passionate about exploring innovative solutions in chemical engineering, leveraging my technical skills to make a meaningful impact. Beyond my academic interests, I have a strong passion for basketball, having competed at the national level. This experience has instilled in me a spirit of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. Additionally, I enjoy photography, capturing moments that reflect the beauty and complexity of the world around us.
In my free time, I indulge in poetry and pleasure writing, painting, and reading, which allow me to express my creativity and unwind. I want the world to see chemical engineering as the vast, diverse, and fun branch it truly is, despite its hardships. Through my work, I aim to highlight the exciting and multifaceted nature of this field, showcasing its potential to drive innovation and improve lives.
I am eager to leverage my engineering skills and creative interests to contribute to innovative projects and meaningful solutions. Open to opportunities that challenge me and allow me to grow, I am committed to making a positive impact through my work.
